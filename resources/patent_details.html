摘要:

Abstract：The invention belongs to the medical apparatus industry, and particularly discloses a health detector based on an intelligent mobile terminal and a detecting method of the health detector. The healthdetector comprises a light transmitting module, a light receiving module, a light processing module and a data analyzing and processing module. Light radiation transmitted by the light transmitting module illuminates the human skin surface, part of light radiation leaves the skin after being scattered and absorbed by skin tissue and enter the light processing module, the light radiation is collected by the light receiving module and converted into skin spectrum data, the skin spectrum data is sent to the data analyzing and processing module to be analyzed and processed, and several pieces of body index information are obtained. The health detector has the advantages that the highly-popularized intelligent mobile terminal is sufficiently used, the instant detection of various body health physical indexes is realized through the collaboration of the light processing module and software, precision and convenience are both achieved, a user can know the physical state without going outside,and the medical resource shortage problem is relieved.
